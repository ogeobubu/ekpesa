Climate and Environment

Ekpesa enjoys a tropical climate with distinct wet and dry seasons. The vegetation is predominantly orchard-bush type, which is thicker in the southern areas near the Onyami River and its tributaries. This climate supports the kingdom's agricultural practices and provides a healthy environment for the Ekpesa people.

Environmental Features

  • Rivers: Onyami River system provides water resources
  • Hills: Kukuruku Hills offer natural defense and scenic beauty
  • Vegetation: Mixed forest and savanna woodland
  • Soil: Fertile loamy soil ideal for agriculture
